# Style Guide

*Templates and learned voice rules. Auto-populated as you archive drafts.*

## Templates

### Project brief email

- Greeting: "Hi <Name>"
- One-line summary of the situation
- 2–4 bullets of next steps
- One specific ask
- Sign-off: "Talk soon" or "Katie"

### P2 call notes

- H1: "<Project> — <Topic> (<Date>)"
- Italic line: attendees + Fathom link
- 3–5 sections of decisions and reasoning
- Closing: open questions or next steps

## Voice rules (learned from drafts)

- Prefer "we" over "I" when speaking on behalf of the team
- Drop hedging words ("just", "kind of") on the second pass
- Lead with the decision; explain after
